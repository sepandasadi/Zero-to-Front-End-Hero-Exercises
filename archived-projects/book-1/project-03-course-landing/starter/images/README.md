## ðŸŽ¨ Image Guidelines

### General Requirements
- **Format:** JPG or WebP
- **Optimization:** Compress all images (use TinyPNG or Squoosh)
- **File size:** Target < 200KB per image
- **Quality:** High quality but optimized

### Specific Guidelines

**Instructor Photo:**
- Professional headshot
- Neutral or office background
- Good lighting
- Smiling, approachable
- Square crop (1:1 ratio)

**Student/Testimonial Photos:**
- Diverse group of people
- Professional but friendly
- Square crop
- Clear faces
- Can use stock photos from Unsplash
