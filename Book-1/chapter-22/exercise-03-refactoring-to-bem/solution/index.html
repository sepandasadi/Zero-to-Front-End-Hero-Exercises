BEM Refactoring Notes

Before → After Mapping

  • .article .header → .article__header
  • .article .header .title → .article__title
  • .article .header .meta .date → .article__meta-date
  • .article .content .intro → .article__intro
  • .article .content .quote → .article__quote
  • .button.social.twitter → .social-button--twitter

Key Improvements

  • Flat selectors: No more deep nesting
  • Low specificity: All selectors have same weight
  • Clear relationships: __ shows element belongs to block
  • Easy modifications: -- clearly indicates variants
  • Independent blocks: Buttons separated into reusable .social-button block

Specificity Comparison

Before: .article .content .quote p (specificity: 0,0,4,0)

After: .article__quote-text (specificity: 0,0,1,0)
