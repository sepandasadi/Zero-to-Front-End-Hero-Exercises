## ðŸ“¸ Image Requirements

### Hero Image
- **Size:** 1920x1080px
- **Format:** JPG
- **File size:** < 500KB
- **Content:** Restaurant exterior, interior, or signature dish

### Menu Images
- **Size:** 800x600px
- **Format:** JPG or WebP
- **File size:** < 200KB each
- **Minimum:** 15 food photos

### Gallery Images
- **Size:** 1200x800px
- **Format:** JPG
- **File size:** < 300KB each
- **Minimum:** 12 photos

### Team Photos
- **Size:** 400x400px (square)
- **Format:** JPG
- **File size:** < 150KB each

## ðŸŽ¨ Image Tips

### Food Photography
- Good lighting is essential
- Use natural light when possible
- Show the full dish
- Plate attractively
- Use props (minimal)

### Restaurant Photos
- Clean, organized space
- Good lighting
- Show unique features
- Include people (optional)
- Multiple angles
