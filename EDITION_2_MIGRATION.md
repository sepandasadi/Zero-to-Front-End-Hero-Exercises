## FAQs

### Q: Are Edition 1 projects "worse"?
**A:** Not at all! Edition 1 projects are excellent learning tools. Edition 2 projects are just more focused and better aligned with the updated book content.

### Q: Should I redo projects I already completed?
**A:** Only if you want to! Your Edition 1 projects are valid portfolio pieces. Consider Edition 2 projects as optional challenges to approach differently.

### Q: Can I mix Edition 1 and Edition 2 projects?
**A:** Yes! There's no hard rule. However, for the most cohesive learning experience, we recommend sticking with Edition 2 projects when following the Edition 2 books.

### Q: Will Edition 1 projects be maintained?
**A:** Edition 1 projects in the archives will remain available and functional, but won't receive updates. Edition 2 projects are the actively maintained versions.

### Q: Are the archived projects production-ready?
**A:** Yes! All archived projects have complete starter files, solutions, requirements, and documentation. They're ready to use as-is.

### Q: Where can I find Edition 1 project solutions?
**A:** In the `archived-projects/` folder. Each project has its complete solution code.
