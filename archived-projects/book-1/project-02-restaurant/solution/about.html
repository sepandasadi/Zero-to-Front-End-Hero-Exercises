Our Story

A passion for authentic Italian cuisine

From Tuscany to Your Table

Bella Trattoria opened its doors in 2010 with a simple mission: to bring the authentic flavors and warm hospitality of Tuscany to our city. Founded by Chef Marco Rossi, who grew up in his grandmother's kitchen in Florence, every dish tells a story of tradition, family, and passion for exceptional food.

What started as a small 20-seat trattoria has grown into one of the city's most beloved Italian restaurants, but we've never lost sight of what matters most: fresh ingredients, time-honored recipes, and treating every guest like family.

Today, we continue to import select ingredients from Italy while sourcing the freshest local produce, meats, and seafood. Our pasta is made fresh daily, our sauces simmer for hours, and every plate is crafted with the same care and attention that Nonna would demand.

Meet Chef Marco Rossi

Executive Chef & Owner

Born and raised in Florence, Italy, Chef Marco learned to cook at his grandmother's side, mastering the traditional recipes passed down through generations. After formal training at the Italian Culinary Institute and working in Michelin-starred restaurants throughout Italy, Marco brought his expertise and passion to America.

"For me, Italian cooking is about more than just food," says Marco. "It's about preserving tradition, honoring the ingredients, and creating moments of joy around the table. Every dish I create is a tribute to my grandmother and the lessons she taught me."

Chef Marco has been featured in Food & Wine Magazine, appeared on the Cooking Channel, and was named "Best Italian Chef" by the local food critic's association three years running.
