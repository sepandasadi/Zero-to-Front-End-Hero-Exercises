Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free plan?

Yes! Our Free plan includes up to 5 team members, 3 projects, and all basic features. It's perfect for small teams and individuals just getting started.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. No long-term contracts or cancellation fees.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and wire transfers for Enterprise plans.

Is my data secure?

Security is our top priority. We use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption, regular security audits, and comply with GDPR, SOC 2, and ISO 27001 standards.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes! Free users get email support (24-hour response time). Pro users get priority support, and Enterprise customers get dedicated support with guaranteed response times.

Can I import data from other tools?

Yes! We offer easy data migration from popular tools like Trello, Asana, Jira, and Monday.com. Our support team can help with large migrations.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?

Yes! We offer 50% discounts for verified nonprofit organizations and educational institutions. Contact our sales team for details.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes! TaskFlow is available on iOS and Android. All features are available on mobile, with offline mode for working on the go.
