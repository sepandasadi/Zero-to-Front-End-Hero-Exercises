Understanding CSS Methodologies: A Complete Guide

November 27, 2024 • Sarah Johnson • 8 min read

CSS methodologies provide structured approaches to writing scalable and maintainable styles. In this comprehensive guide, we'll explore the most popular methodologies and when to use them.

When projects grow beyond a few pages, unstructured CSS becomes increasingly difficult to manage. Naming conflicts arise, specificity wars begin, and simple changes can have unexpected side effects throughout your application.

"Good CSS architecture is about finding the right balance between reusability, maintainability, and flexibility."

— Nicole Sullivan

Popular methodologies like BEM, OOCSS, and SMACSS each solve these problems in different ways. Understanding their strengths helps you choose the right approach for your project.
