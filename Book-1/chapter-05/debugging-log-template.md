## How to Use This Log

When you encounter a bug or problem:

1. **Document the problem** before randomly trying fixes
2. **Track what you try** - even failed attempts teach you something
3. **Record the solution** - you might face this again!
4. **Note the lesson** - what will you remember next time?
5. **Review weekly** - look for patterns in your bugs

### Universal Debugging Wisdom:

1. **Read the error message** - It usually tells you exactly what's wrong
2. **Check the console** - Look for errors and warnings
3. **Use console.log()** - Print values to see what's happening
4. **Isolate the problem** - Comment out code to narrow down where the issue is
5. **Google the exact error** - Someone has likely faced this before
6. **Check your spelling** - Typos are the #1 cause of bugs
7. **Validate your HTML/CSS** - Use W3C validators
8. **Test in different browsers** - Browser differences are real
9. **Take a break** - Fresh eyes often spot the issue immediately
10. **Explain it to a rubber duck** - Talking through the problem helps
